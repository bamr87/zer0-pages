About zer0-pages

zer0-pages is a next-generation content management system that combines the power of Django's robust backend with Jekyll's lightning-fast static site generation.

Powered by AI throughout, zer0-pages automates content creation, repository management, documentation generation, and site deployment—all with zero configuration friction.

Our Mission

We believe content creation should be effortless. With zer0-pages, you get:

  • AI-First Architecture: Every feature enhanced by AI integration
  • Zero Friction: Works immediately with minimal configuration
  • Privacy First: GDPR/CCPA compliant from day one
  • Open Source: Free and open for everyone
