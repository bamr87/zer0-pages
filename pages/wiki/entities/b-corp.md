# B Corp

## Overview

A **B Corporation** ("B Corp") is a for-profit company certified by the nonprofit **B Lab** as meeting verified standards of social and environmental performance, public accountability, and transparency. In [[wiki/sources/wizard-topples-capitalist-dominance|the wizard post]] B Corp certification is Exhibit A in the argument that "'shareholder value plus ethics'" is not a contradiction — a working, real-world instance of [[wiki/concepts/stakeholder-capitalism|stakeholder capitalism]]. It is linked in the post's FAQ as [B Corp certification](https://www.bcorporation.net/).

## Key Facts

- **What it certifies:** verified social/environmental performance plus legal accountability to stakeholders beyond shareholders.
- **Why the post cites it:** as evidence — together with the Embankment Project for Inclusive Capitalism (EPIC) — that "long-horizon shareholder returns are *positively* correlated with stakeholder welfare," and that the apparent contradiction "only appears under quarterly-earnings tunnel vision."
- **Positioning:** a concrete framework a company can actually adopt, as opposed to an abstract ideal.
