# GLEIF

## Overview

GLEIF — the **Global Legal Entity Identifier Foundation** — maintains the **Legal Entity Identifier (LEI)**, described by [[wiki/sources/wizard-topples-capitalist-dominance|the wizard post]] as "global, free, open corporate identity data." It is one of the three public data sources feeding [[wiki/concepts/radical-transparency|the transparency pipeline]] (alongside [[wiki/entities/sec-edgar|SEC EDGAR]] and OpenCorporates). Its site is [gleif.org](https://www.gleif.org/).

## Key Facts

- **What the LEI is:** a standardized, globally unique identifier for legally distinct entities that participate in financial transactions — the "who is this company, really" key that lets filings from different sources be joined.
- **Why the post names it:** open, free identity data is the connective tissue of a transparency pipeline — it lets an "open ledger" reconcile records about the same entity across [[wiki/entities/sec-edgar|EDGAR]], OpenCorporates, and other feeds.
- **Positioning:** grouped with EDGAR and OpenCorporates as "nothing proprietary, nothing arcane" — swappable, public infrastructure.
